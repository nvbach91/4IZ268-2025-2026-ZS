Structures
Chest
Chests are laying all around the map in Megabonk. Opening on of these will cost you certain amount of gold, which increases the more chests you open (starting at 30 gold). From such chest you can receive one of many items present in Megabonk.
Golden Chest
This is special kind of normal chest. You do not need to pay any gold in order to open this chest.
Pot
Pots are the most frequent thing found in the maps. By breaking pots player can receive rewards such as gold, silver or EXP for their character. Additionally, it is possible to receive temporary buffs such as heal or time-freeze.
Shrine
Pretty common structure in maps. After standing near it (within the designated circle and charging its bar to the fullest), you'll receive a permanent stat-boost to your character.
Moai
You can "consult" this statue in order to receive a free permanent character stat-boost (one of three presented can be chosen).
Magnet
By pressing/activating magnet, all the EXP currently laying on map will be "sucked" by the players character, receiving massive amounts of EXP.
Curse statue
Activating curse statue causes the game's difficulty to increase by a certain percentage. This means the rewards will be higher as well as the difficulty.
Jukebox
Plays some music!
Shady Guy
This guy gives player chance to trade gold for a valuable item of their choosing (3 choices). Disappears once trade is completed.
Boss Curse
Activating this statue increases the amount of bosses summoned by the Boss Portal by one.
Boss Portal
Activating this portal spawn the final boss(es). After defeating the final boss(es), the level finishes, all normal enemies will despawn and overtime will begin. Also, if this is your second play through of the level a portal will open, which allows you to continue to the next level/map.
