Forest Map
This map is unlock by default. It's a green place full of trees, birds, and great vibes. Also, everything will try to kill you. It has many cliffs and hills, few ruins are scattered around the map as well.
Desert Map
Lots of sprawling open areas, with large rocks, cacti, desert-themed buildings, and appropriately themed enemies. Mechanically, there are a few distinct features of this map, namely tornados, and enemies that deal poison damage (Scorpions). Expect new bosses within this map, such as Scorpionussy.
