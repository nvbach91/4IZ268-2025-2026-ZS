What is MegaBonk?
Introduction
Megabonk is a 2025 roguelike survival game developed and published by solo developer vedinad. Described as a 3D Vampire Survivors–like, it features procedurally generated maps, automatic combat against hordes of enemies, and character progression through randomized upgrades. The game was released for Windows and Linux on September 18, 2025 (source: Wikipedia).
Gameplay
Megabonk is a third-person vampire survival like game, which, unlike Vampire Survivors has very simple 3D graphics. Players can only affect the characters movement (WASD), since the fighting is being done automatically, and it's power scales according to the upgrades player finds or chooses. Such upgrades can be either found on the map and collected by interacting with structures associated with them (white exclamation mark above), chosen as a reward for leveling-up (reached by collecting EXP) or chosen/received from entities selling them. Last way to receive any upgrades is to defeat bosses.
Reception
Players and critical reception has been very positive since the game's release. Currently, Megabonk garners 97% (as of November 29th) - "Extremely Positive" player rating and 8.3 - Generally Favorable rating on famous game-critic site/forum Metacritic. Megabonk was also nominated in a Game Awards category "Best Debut Indie Game" but the game's developer rejected this nomination.
