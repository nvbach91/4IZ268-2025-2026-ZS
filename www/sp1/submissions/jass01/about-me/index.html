About me

I'm Samuel - a web developer, student, and rider who finds freedom on two wheels. When I'm not buried in code, I'm out chasing quiet roads, mountain passes, or just a good view with friends. This blog is where I mix both worlds - sharing my passion for motorcycles, travel, and the little adventures that make life more than just routine.

My motorcycling journey

My motorcycling journey started about 1.5 years ago, when I decided to get my A2 license (EU standard - bikes up to 35KW). This was kinda of a spur of the moment decision, as I was thinking about unique ways I could see the world, and motorcycling seemed like a fun and adventerous way to travel. After getting my license, I was looking for bikes that would fit my requirements, but not break the bank at the same time. My budget was quite tight, so options were limited, but after looking for about 2 months I ended up finding a 1999 Kawasaki ER5 and instantly fell in love with it. The bike was in rough condition. The tires, brakes, oil, and filters needed to be replaced, but the frame and engine were intact.

Unfortunately, after getting the bike I ended up breaking my leg on my scooter, and was out of the season for the whole summer. Which is why my adventures with the bike are very limited, and most of the trip tips I share are trips I am still planning myself. However, I look forward to undergoing them in the next season and enjoying every moment on my bike!
