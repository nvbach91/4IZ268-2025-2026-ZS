FAQ

Is there crossplay in Deep Rock Galactic?

Yes - but with limitations. Xbox / Microsoft Store players can crossplay with each other and with Windows Store PC users.

Steam players can only play with other Steam players.

Is Deep Rock Galactic single-player friendly?

Yes! You can play solo with Bosco, your drone companion. He can mine, fight, and revive you.

Does Deep Rock Galactic have PvP?

No. DRG is fully co-op PvE. The devs have stated that PvP doesn’t fit the spirit of the game.

Is progression shared between missions?

Yes. All gold, minerals, upgrades, and cosmetics are permanently saved. Even failed missions grant partial rewards.

Does the game support mods?

Yes. DRG has full mod support through Mod.io, with approved, sandbox, and verified mods depending on how they affect gameplay.

Is Deep Rock Galactic cross-save?

Not fully.

Xbox → Windows Store: Yes, progress carries over.

Steam ↔ Xbox/PlayStation: No, saves are not shared between platforms.

Can I play Deep Rock Galactic with a controller on pc?

Absolutely. DRG has full controller support on all platforms.
