Welcome to Music Club

Red lightning nights, live music and community

About the Club

Music Club is a place for bands, solo singers and music fans who want to enjoy live shows, meet new people and create unforgettable evenings. We organise concerts, jam sessions and workshops for beginners and experienced musicians.

Our goal is to support local artists and give them a stage with good sound, friendly audience and strong atmosphere. Everyone who loves music is welcome – listeners, performers and volunteers.

What You Will Find Here

  • Live Events

    Regular concerts with rock, pop, indie and electronic bands. Check the events section for the next red lightning night.

  • Bands & Solo Singers

    We work with local bands and solo artists. You can apply for a gig or join as a new member of an existing project.

  • Club Team

    A small group of people who take care of sound, lights, programme and promotion so the whole club works smoothly.

  • News & Updates

    Announcements about new events, photo galleries from previous nights and short stories from behind the stage.
