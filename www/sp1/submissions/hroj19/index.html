Welcome to VŠE Falcons Prague

VŠE Falcons Prague is the official university ice hockey team of the Prague University of Economics and Business. The team competes in the University Ice Hockey League (ULLH) and brings together students from different faculties who share passion for hockey, teamwork and fair play.

On this website you can find the current roster, results of played games, basic player statistics and contact information for the 2025/26 season.

About this website

This website was created as a student project for the course Web Technologies at the Prague University of Economics and Business. Its purpose is to demonstrate the structure and design of a simple static website for a university sports team.

All data on this website (results, statistics and contacts) are illustrative and do not represent the official communication of the team or the university.
