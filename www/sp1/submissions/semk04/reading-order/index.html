Reading Order

The Throne of Glass timeline is mostly straightforward, but there is a major debate regarding when to read the prequel novellas, The Assassin's Blade.
Below are the three most popular ways to experience the series.

img

Option 1 - The Chronological Order

Read The Assassin's Blade first.
This sets the stage for Celaena's past before the main events of Throne of Glass begin. You get the full emotional context immediately.

Option 2 - The Romantic Order

Read the prequel after Crown of Midnight (2nd book).
This provides a break in the action after the plot twists of Book 2 and gives you the necessary backstory right before it becomes crucial in Book 3.
This reading order is also recommended by the author.

Option 3 - The Impact Order

Read the prequel after Heir of Fire (3rd book).
Known as the "emotional damage" route. Reading the prequel here maximizes the impact of the events in Queen of Shadows, tying the past and present together perfectly.

Tandem Read

What is the Tandem Read?

Empire of Storms and Tower of Dawn take place at the exact same time but follow different characters.

Because Empire of Storms ends on a massive cliffhanger, many readers choose to read both books simultaneously, switching chapters to keep the timeline synced and avoid the suspense.

TOD follows the story of Chaol and his recovery after the events of the prior book (Queen of Shadows) while EOS follows the story of Aelin and her companions. When something happens in one book, the aftermath is shown in the other.
