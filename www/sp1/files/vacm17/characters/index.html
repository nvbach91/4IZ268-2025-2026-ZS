Characters
Fox
Fox is one of the two starting characters in Megabonk. It is available from the beginning of the game and does not require any unlock conditions. Fox specializes in ranged combat, starting with the Firestaff, and gains +1% Luck per level.
Role Health Luck Special ability
Ranged DPS 100HP 5% Gain 2% Luck per level

Sir Oofie
Sir Oofie
Sir Oofie is one of the two starting characters in Megabonk. It is available immediately and requires no unlock conditions. Sir Oofie favors frontline, defensive play with the Sword and gains Armor each level through its RNG blessing.
Role Health Armour Special ability
Tank 150HP 17% Gain 1% Armour per level
Megachad
Megachad is an unlockable character in Megabonk. It requires upgrading the Damage Tome to level 7. Megachad starts with the Aura weapon, which specializes in control and defense.
Role Health - Special ability
Tank 120HP - Flex: Stuns enemies and prevents damage. Gain 2.5% Damage when you flex.
Megachad
Calcium
Calcium
Calcium is an unlockable character in Megabonk. It requires defeating 1,000 Skeletons to become available. Calcium uses the Bone as its starting weapon and has a unique blessing tied to movement speed.
Role Health Move Speed Special ability
Speed-based bruiser 100HP 1.2x Speeds-up over time, speed halved when damage is taken
CL4NK
CL4NK is an unlockable character in Megabonk. It is unlocked by completing Forest Tier 1. CL4NK starts with the Revolver and specializes in critical hits.
Role Health Move Speed Special ability
Ranged DPS 40HP + (60 shield) 1.1x Gain 1% Crit Chance per level

Monke
Monke
Monke is an unlockable character in Megabonk. He is freed by exploring the Forest, finding the Gold Key, and unlocking his cage. Monke starts with the Bananarang and has both mobility and tank traits.
Role Health Damage Special ability
Brawler 135HP 1.2x Climb up walls. Gain +2 Max HP per level
Athena
Athena is a unlockable character. She wields the Aegis and her passive is “Lock In” — she deals more damage when at low HP, and gains Thorns per level. Unlock by obtaining the Thorns Tome to level 9 and buying the Aegis.
Role Health Passive Effect Special Ability
Versatile / Burst 125HP Thorns ↑ per level Damage scales up when HP is low
Athena
Bush
Bush
Bush is an unlockable long-range character. He starts with the Sniper Rifle and his passive “Bullseye” marks enemies randomly — shooting marked enemies causes an explosion nearby. Also gains +1% Crit Damage per level. Unlock by finding and defeating Bush in the Forest and buying the Sniper Rifle.
Role Health Crit Damage Gain/Level Special Ability
Sniper / Ranged DPS - +1% per level Explosive crit on marked enemies
Ogre
Ogre is an unlockable character that uses the Axe. His passive “Warrior” increases damage by 1.5% per level. To unlock: kill 15,000 Goblins and buy the Axe.
Role Health Damage Gain/Level Special Ability
Melee / Bruiser 140HP +1.5% per level Scales well on long runs, heavy hits
Ogre
Tony McZoom
Tony McZoom
Tony McZoom is an unlockable character starting with the Wireless Dagger. His passive “Zap” lets him charge an electric attack by moving; he also gains +2% pickup range per level. Unlock by completing 2 Challenges and buying the Wireless Dagger.
Role Health Pickup Range Gain/Level Special Ability
Hybrid / Agile — +2% per level Electric charge attack while moving
