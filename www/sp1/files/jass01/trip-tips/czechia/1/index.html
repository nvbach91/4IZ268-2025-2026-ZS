Bohemian Peaks & Border Roads — 4–6 Days of Czech Mountain Riding and Historic Towns

Discover the best of Czechia on a compact but adventure-rich motorcycle loop that links the country’s most beautiful landscapes. Ride through rolling Bohemian hills, weave along the curvy border roads of Šumava and the Jeseníky mountains, explore sandstone towers in Český ráj, and stop in charming historic towns along the way. This trip combines smooth tarmac, quiet countryside, and panoramic viewpoints, making it a perfect mid-length escape for riders who want to experience the natural diversity of Czechia without crossing borders. From fairy-tale castles to mountain ridges and pristine national parks, every day delivers fresh scenery and enjoyable bends tailored for two wheels.

Trip details

  • Distance: approx. 900–1,200 km
  • Countries: Czechia
  • Type: Sightseeing / Mountain & Countryside Touring
  • Estimated days: 4–6
  • Suitable for: Riders who enjoy national parks, border mountain roads, and quiet scenic routes

Roadmap

  1. Prague → Karlštejn → Plzeň
  2. Plzeň → Šumava National Park → Lipno
  3. Lipno → Český Krumlov → Tábor
  4. Tábor → Hradec Králové → Český ráj (Prachovské skály)
  5. Český ráj → Krkonoše Mountains (Špindlerův Mlýn)
  6. Krkonoše → Jeseníky (Červenohorské sedlo) → Olomouc
  7. Olomouc → Litomyšl → return to Prague
