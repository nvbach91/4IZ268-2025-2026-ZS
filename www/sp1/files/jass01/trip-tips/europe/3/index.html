Pan-European Epic — 16–20 Days Across Peaks, Coastlines, and Iconic European Routes

This Pan-European Epic is a grand journey for travelers who want to experience the full spectrum of Europe in one sweeping adventure. From the medieval towns of Central Europe to the soaring passes of the Alps, the sunlit shores of the Adriatic, and the dramatic landscapes of the Balkans, this route blends legendary riding with rich cultural discovery. Ascend world-famous mountain roads like the Stelvio Pass, wander historic old towns from Verona to Dubrovnik, and trace the coastline all the way to Montenegro’s towering fjord-like bays. Whether you choose the Alpine-focused path or extend the journey north toward Norway’s majestic fjords, this long-distance expedition delivers Europe at its most diverse and dramatic. Perfect for riders seeking an ambitious multi-country odyssey filled with unforgettable roads, natural wonders, and postcard-worthy views at every turn.

Trip details

  • Distance: 4,000 to 6,000 km (depending on variant)
  • Countries: Czechia, Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Italy, Slovenia, Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Montenegro (optional extension: Norway)
  • Type: Sightseeing / Alpine & Coastal Touring
  • Estimated days: 16–20
  • Suitable for: Experienced riders who enjoy long distances, major mountain passes, coastal routes, and varied terrain

Roadmap

  1. Prague → Český Budějovice → cross into Germany
  2. Regensburg → Munich (Bavaria)
  3. Garmisch-Partenkirchen → cross into Austria
  4. Innsbruck → continue to Reschen Pass
  5. Stilfser Joch / Stelvio Pass (Italy) — epic climb and descent
  6. Bormio → Lake Garda (Riva / Riva del Garda)
  7. Verona → Venice (optional coastal segment) → Trieste
  8. Enter Slovenia: Koper → Ljubljana
  9. Zagreb (Croatia) → Plitvice Lakes National Park
  10. Zadar → Split → Dubrovnik (Dalmatian coast)
  11. Kotor (Montenegro) → Budva → cross into Bosnia or return via Croatia
  12. Return: Zagreb → Maribor (Slovenia) → Graz → Vienna → Brno → Prague
