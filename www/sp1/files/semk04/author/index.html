Author

Sarah J. Maas

Full name: Sarah Janet Maas
Date of birth: 5th May 1986

Sarah J. Maas is an American fantasy author. As of 2024, she has sold more than 75 million copies of her books and her work has been translated into 40 languages.

Maas graduated from Hamilton College in 2008 with a degree in creative writing and minor in religious studies.

She made her debut in 2012 with Throne of Glass that she had started writing at the age of sixteen. First, she posted several chapters of the novel then titled Queen of Glass on fanfiction archive site, where it became one of the most popular stories.

Author's other work

A Court of Thorns & Roses


ACOTAR is a fantasy-romance series that begins with a loose retelling of Beauty and the Beast, then expands into a saga of magic, war, politics and love.
The story follows Feyre Archeron, a mortal huntress who is taken into the fae lands of Prythian as punishment for killing a fae wolf. What begins as captivity transforms into a journey of empowerment as Feyre uncovers ancient curses, dangerous courts, and forces that threaten both the human and fae realms.


Books in the series:
  • A Court of Thorns and Roses
  • A Court of Mist and Fury
  • A Court of Wings and Ruin
  • A Court of Frost and Starlight
  • A Court of Silver Flames

Crescent City


Crescent City is an adult urban-fantasy series set in a modern, magic-infused metropolis where angels, demons, Fae, shifters, and humans coexist under a fragile peace. The story centers on Bryce Quinlan, a half-Fae party girl whose life is shattered by a brutal tragedy, drawing her into a complex investigation that exposes corruption, ancient power, and dangerous truths buried within the city.
Blending mystery, action, romance, and emotional depth, the series delivers a cinematic world of neon-lit streets, ancient magic, and high-stakes intrigue.


Books in the series:
  • Crescent City - House of Earth and Blood
  • Crescent City - House of Sky and Breath
  • Crescent City - House of Flame and Shadow
