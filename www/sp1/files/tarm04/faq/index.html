For Students

Individual Lessons

  • Payment: Made in advance as a subscription starting from 4 lessons.
  • Cancellation: Not charged if canceled at least 2 hours before the start.
  • Late Cancel: Late cancellations or no-shows are fully charged.
  • Rescheduling: Allowed within subscription period, subject to teacher’s availability.
  • Validity: Subscriptions are valid for 60 days from first use.

Group Classes

  • Payment: Made monthly or for the entire level/course at once.
  • Attendance: If 4 out of 6 participants cancel in advance, the class is moved to the closest date.
  • Recordings: All lessons are recorded and sent to the group chat for review.
  • Refunds: Missed classes are not refundable, but all materials are provided.

Speaking Club

  • Payment: Made per session, no later than 8 hours before start.
  • Attendance: If paid but not attended, the spot is considered used.
  • Environment: Respectful communication and an inclusive environment are mandatory.
