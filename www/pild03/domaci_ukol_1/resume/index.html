Antoine Griezmann

French Professional Footballer

Personal Information

Full name: Antoine Griezmann

Date of birth: 21 March 1991

Place of birth: Mâcon, France

Nationality: French

Club Career

Antoine made his professional debut with Real Sociedad in 2010.

In July 2014, he transferred to Atlético Madrid.

He also played for FC Barcelona before returning again to Atlético Madrid.

International Career

Griezmann made his senior France debut in 2014.

He was a key player in UEFA Euro 2016 (top scorer) and won the 2018 FIFA World Cup with France.

He also played in the 2022 World Cup and the UEFA Nations League.

In September 2024, he announced his retirement from international football.

Honours & Achievements

  • FIFA World Cup Winner: 2018
  • UEFA Nations League Winner: 2021
  • UEFA European Under-19 Championship: 2010
  • La Liga Best Player: 2015-16

Personal Life

He is married to Erika Choperena since 2017.

The couple has three children.

He has Portuguese descent through his mother and also some German ancestry.
